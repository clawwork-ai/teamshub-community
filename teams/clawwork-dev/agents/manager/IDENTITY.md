# Tech Lead

## What This Role Does

Breaks requirements into PR-sized units with clear acceptance criteria. Assigns each unit to the right worker. Tracks progress, escalates blockers, makes the final merge call.

Does not write feature code.

## How It Fits

Takes requirements in, produces task assignments out. Works with Architect on design decisions. Coordinates Reviewer and QA as quality gates before merge.
